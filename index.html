Hello, I'm
Ai Ling.

An interior designer based in Lisbon, working at the intersection of neuroarchitecture, sensory design, and the quiet beauty of everyday life.

Since childhood, I've been fascinated by how our surroundings influence mood, thought, and connection. Living in London, Berlin, and now Lisbon has shaped my eye for calm, characterful spaces that hold warmth amid the rhythm of daily life.

Noetic Spaces was born from my interest in neuroarchitecture and sensory design, and from working alongside neurodivergent individuals and families. My approach balances stimulation with restfulness, guided by my Singaporean upbringing and the principles of Feng Shui — the grounding power of flow, natural textures, and earth tones.

Beyond form and function, I see design as a dialogue between the visible and the unseen. Through collaborations with emerging artists, locally sourced materials, and independent makers, I create spaces that nurture belonging while honouring the diverse voices and stories that shape our shared environments.

I look forward to designing a space that feels grounding every time you step inside — and a place that reflects who you are and how you wish to live.

Staging
that sells
stories.

Property staging is the art of helping buyers imagine their life in a space. We don't just style rooms — we craft a feeling of possibility, warmth, and belonging that lingers long after the viewing ends.

With roots in neuroarchitecture and sensory design, our staging considers how people experience space subconsciously — guiding attention, evoking calm, and creating the emotional conditions for a sale.

How it works

01
Consultation

We visit the property, assess what's there, and discuss your goals — timeline, buyer profile, and budget.

02
Proposal

A tailored staging plan, including what stays, what goes, what we bring in, and which spaces to prioritise.

03
Styling Day

We arrive with our curated inventory and spend the day transforming the property — typically completed in one session.

04
Photography

We coordinate with your photographer or can recommend trusted partners for listing and marketing materials.
